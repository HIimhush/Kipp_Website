Why Ribby?

We chose to create Ribby because we wanted a companion that feels approachable and neutral. Ribby was designed to be a supportive presence—someone who listens without judgment. Whether you're struggling with a tough concept or just need a moment to breathe, Ribby is here to help you navigate your learning journey with patience and understanding.

Compatibility

Kipp is designed to work seamlessly across a wide range of devices, ensuring that older models are not left behind. Whether you're using the latest flagship or a device from a few years ago, Kipp adapts to provide a smooth learning experience without compromising performance.

Great Accessibility

We believe learning should be accessible to everyone. Kipp includes features like high-contrast modes, screen reader support, and adjustable text sizes to ensure that every user, regardless of their abilities, can navigate and learn effectively.

About Us

Our team is passionate about education and technology. We started with a simple idea: make learning fun and accessible. "Shaped as a watchful frog" isn't just a design choice; it's a symbol of our commitment to being there for every student, guiding them through their educational journey.
